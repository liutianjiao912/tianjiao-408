$$
\int_{\frac{\pi}{6}}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} dx \int_{\sin x}^1 f(x, y) dy = （ ）．
$$

（C）$ \int_0^{\frac{1}{2}} dy \int_{\frac{\pi}{6}}^{\arcsin y} f(x, y) dx $  
（D）$ \int_0^{\frac{1}{2}} dy \int_{\arcsin y}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} f(x, y) dx $
